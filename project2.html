Project Overview

This project was a collaboration between 4 students to create a website on a topic of choice; our group chose to design a coffee shop website.

We planned and implemented a Gantt chart that broke down every participant's roles and the timeline for when the corresponding goals/prototypes would be completed.

Project Planning for Fall 2024:

  1. Statement of Work
  2. System Concept Statement
  3. Research and Design - Interactive features, colors/themes, layout, products offered
  4. Storyboard - Drawn to show rough menu ideas

Prototype Development

Every two weeks we needed to have the next prototype with the new features implemented and record a video showcase with commentary explaining the changes and any issues or concerns that arose during that prototype stage.
